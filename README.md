#  Amazon Sales Data Analysis

**Technologies:** `SQL` • `Power BI` • `Excel` • `Data Cleaning` • `Customer Segmentation` • `Business Insights`  

End-to-end analysis of 15,000+ Amazon orders (₹1.12B revenue) to uncover trends, customer behavior, returns, and regional performance. Demonstrates skills in **SQL, Power BI, KPI design, and data storytelling**.

---

##  Project Overview
Amazon faced challenges like fluctuating sales, high returns, uneven regional performance, and difficulty identifying high-value customers.  

**Goal:** Turn raw data into actionable business insights via an interactive Power BI dashboard.

**Workflow:**  

---

##  Dataset
- Orders, Product & Category info  
- Customer & Ratings Data  
- State-level sales & returns  

---

##  Power BI Dashboard
- Revenue & Average Order Value (AOV) KPIs  
- Monthly & Seasonal Sales Trends  
- Category-wise Sales & Returns  
- State-wise Performance Map  
- Top Customers & Retention Insights  
- Ratings Distribution  

---

##  Key Insights
- **Sales Trends:** Peaks in May, August, December; Q3 & Q4 strongest  
- **Category Performance:** Beauty & Books = 33% returns; Electronics = revenue leader  
- **Customer Insights:** Top 25% generate ~62% of total revenue  
- **Regional Insights:** Strongest = Maharashtra, Karnataka, Delhi; Weak = North-East  

---

##  Recommendations & Impact
- Reduce returns via quality checks  
- Allocate inventory efficiently  
- Retention campaigns for top customers  
- Improve logistics in underperforming regions  
- Increase marketing during seasonal peaks  

**Impact:** Clear revenue drivers, improved forecasting, stronger retention strategy, region-wise planning, better product-level decisions
